Google Maps Icons to OsmAnd GPX

Convert Google My Maps placemarks into OsmAnd GPX waypoints with similar icons where possible.

What it does

Convert Google My Maps waypoint icons into similar OsmAnd waypoint icons. This page focuses on placemarks and icon mapping.

  1. Export your Google My Maps data as KML or KMZ.
  2. Drop the file here.
  3. Review detected icon mappings and download the waypoint GPX.
Unknown icons are reported so the mapping table can be improved later.
