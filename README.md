## About me

I'm Eugene, CMO at **OsmAnd B.V.**, the company behind OsmAnd — an offline maps and navigation app based on OpenStreetMap data.

My work is not limited to marketing. I also work closely with users, communities, partners, documentation, app testing, and support. I help connect product development with real user feedback, so OsmAnd can become more useful for travelers, drivers, cyclists, hikers, and outdoor enthusiasts around the world.

## What I do at OsmAnd

- Lead marketing activities and product communication
- Work with OsmAnd users, communities, and partners
- Help maintain and improve [OsmAnd documentation](https://osmand.net/docs/intro)
- Test OsmAnd apps on Android, iOS, Android Auto, and CarPlay
- Collect and analyze user feedback from support channels
- Help with QA, bug reports, feature requests, and release communication
- Support the promotion of offline maps, privacy-focused navigation, and OpenStreetMap-based tools

## OsmAnd

OsmAnd is an offline maps and navigation application for Android, iOS, and web. It is designed for people who need reliable maps without depending on a constant internet connection.

Useful links:

- Website: [osmand.net](https://osmand.net/)
- Documentation: [osmand.net/docs](https://osmand.net/docs/intro)
- GitHub organization: [github.com/osmandapp](https://github.com/osmandapp)
- Web map: [osmand.net/map](https://osmand.net/map)
